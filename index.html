How It Works

Four Steps to Your Next Adventure

No planning needed. No decision fatigue. Just scratch, reveal, and go.

1

Get Your Card

A mystery card is always waiting on your home screen. Its rarity is unknown until you scratch.

2

Scratch to Reveal

Scratch the foil with your finger to reveal a personalised challenge designed for you and your partner.

3

Adventure Together

Complete the challenge before the deadline. Push your comfort zone, create memories, grow closer.

4

Capture & Collect

Snap a photo, add it to your scrapbook. Track your streak. Build your adventure collection over time.

Features

Built for Couples Who Want More

Every feature is designed to bring you closer, not just keep you busy.

AI-Powered Challenges

Powered by Google Gemini. Challenges adapt to your city, interests, comfort zone, exclusions, and season. No two couples get the same experience.

Shared Scrapbook

Both partners snap their perspective. Flip between views. Export as a polaroid-style card or order a printed book of your adventures.

Streaks & Sparks

120 daily conversation sparks to keep the connection alive. Build your streak. Hit milestones at 7, 30, 90 and 365 days.

Our World

Love notes, bucket lists, countdown dates, photo mosaics, and monthly memories. Your shared digital space as a couple.

Comfort Zone Engine

5 sliders map your comfort zone. The app deliberately pushes 1-2 dimensions each challenge. Gentle at first, bolder as you grow.

Dare a Couple

Completed a challenge? Flick it to another couple as a dare. Add a custom message and set stakes. The couple-to-couple viral loop.
